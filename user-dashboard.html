Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process my order?

Most orders are completed within 24-48 hours. Dry cleaning may take 2-3 business days.

Can I track my order status?

Yes! You can track your order status in real-time from your dashboard or the "My Orders" section.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. Contact us within 24 hours of delivery and we'll make it right.
